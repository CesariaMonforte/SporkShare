Who SporkShare is for?

Sporkshare is a food social media app designed to help individuals with dietary preferences and needs. It aims to reduce the stress of restaurant searching while keeping user dietary requirements as a top priority. Its efficient filters make food discovery simple and easy. Users can also share their experiences, create custom collections, and connect with others who have similar dietary needs on SporkShare.
